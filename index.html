Kimin Yun

I am a senior researcher at Visual Intelligence Lab at the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI) in South Korea.

At ETRI, I am involved in national R&D projects focusing on both foundational technologies and applied research of computer vision and machine learning. My research interest is to improve human life through smarter, more beneficial vision systems, aiming to create practical applications.

I completed an integrated Master's and Ph.D. program in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, under the supervision of Prof. Jin Young Choi, in 2017. I received my Bachelor's degree from the Seoul National University in 2010.
